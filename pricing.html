The Japan Habba Trust is approved as a charitable trust under sections 12A and 80G of the Income Tax Act since December 08, 2016; thus, donations to the trust will 50% tax free. A receipt with the certificate numbers will automatically be sent to the provided email address on receipt of your contribution. Please note that no invoice can be provided.

Please note that there is no certain scheme for donations at the moment. While the possible tiers for donations are under discussion, we currently only accept donations which are completely voluntary and can be of any free amount by the user.
