About The Event

Japan Habba Tech Fair is a India-Japanese technical conference held annually in IISc Bangalore. It is colocated with the Japan Habba cultural event, and seeks to bring together an audience of enthusiastic people from academia and industry, interested in Indo-Japan collaboration.

Where

Faculty Hall, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore

When
