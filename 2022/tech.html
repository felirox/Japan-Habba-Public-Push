Hello and welcome to the second ever Japan Habba Tech Fair to be held at Faculty Hall, IISc Bangalore on 2 Feburary, 2019. It is a collaboration between three bodies — Japan External Trade Organization, IEEE-IISc and The Indo-Japanese Chamber of Commerce and Industry!

We have a day-long program with an array of interesting speakers from both academia and industry, the details of which are given below.

  • We have 7 participating companies who are interested in recruiting students, not just in engineering.
  • We have speakers affilited with Japanese Universities such as The University of Tokyo.
  • Speakers will be available for interaction during the breaks.
  • Plenty of opportunities available for those with no Japanese knowledge.
  • Internships opportunities are also available at some of the visiting companies!

All registrants will get lunch, tea and snacks, welcome kit and participation certificates.
